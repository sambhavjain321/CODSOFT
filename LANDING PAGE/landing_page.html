VARIETIES

Pome

a pome is a type of fruit produced by flowering plants in the subtribe Malinae of the family Rosaceae.

Aggregate fruit

The individual fruits within an aggregate fruit can be of different types, such as berries, drupes, or follicles.

Mangoes

One of the yellow fruits in the list of tropical fruits that is a drupe is the mango. Mangoes are rich sources of vitamins C .

Berries

Berries are among the most popular types of fruit because of their varied taste, color, and texture.

Plums

Consuming 3 plums will give you 19 mg of vitamin C and 2.7 g of fiber which is 30% and 12% of your daily requirements.

Golden Delicious

A variety with comparatively longer shelf life, it is crispy, juicy and has thick greenish-white flesh which turns golden upon ripening. It is available till January.
